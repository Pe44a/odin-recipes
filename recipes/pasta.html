Pasta recipe

So tasty!

Recipe

  • 8 ounces dry pasta
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Rinse with cold water.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 5 to 7 minutes or until still slightly less than al dente; drain.
  3. Toss with olive oil. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use. To reheat, bring a large pot of water to a boil, add pasta and cook until hot, 1 to 2 minutes.
